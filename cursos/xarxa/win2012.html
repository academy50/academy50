NETWORK INSTALLATION AND ADMINISTRATION COURSE (SERVER 2012)

goals

Prepare the student in the design, installation and administration of a network. At the end of the course the student will be able to manage, control and manipulate a LAN-type network.

Methodology

All our courses use an active and practical methodology from day one. Personalized classes, with a teacher, face-to-face in Tarrega and adapted to each student, with totally flexible schedules.

Schedules

Totally flexible, modifying them according to your needs.

Duration

The duration of the course is APPROXIMATELY 40 hours. These hours are estimated as a reference, not all students need the same hours, it all depends on the ability of each one to assimilate the syllabus.
